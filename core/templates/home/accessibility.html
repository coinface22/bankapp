Our Commitment to Accessibility

We are committed to making our website usable and inclusive to all people. In creating and updating our website, we strive to make sure it is compatible with accessibility assistance software and tools.

Our commitment to help

If you have specific questions, concerns, or are experiencing difficulties accessing our site, reach out to VACU’s Member Services at 804-323-6800.

Third-party applications

Our website links to many third-party sites, some affiliated to VACU and others, such as social media pages, that are not. Many of these sites are not controlled by VACU and therefore we cannot control the way content is displayed on these sites.
